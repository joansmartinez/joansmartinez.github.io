About Me

HI, I am a rising senior at Columbia University's School of Engineering and Applied Science from New Jersey studying Computer Science with a focus on Intelligent Systems. Aside from being interested in technology, specifically things dealing with linguistics and the mind, and it's applications to our world, the majority of my passions lie elsewhere. Incredibly fascinatated by Latin music and dance specifically Bachata and Merengue from my native Dominican Republic. An avid fan of track and field and everything running related from new shoes, stats, to the latest technologies and race results. I have also recently gained a large interest in the field of Analytical Philosophy over Continental and how the ideas created by Bertrand Russel and Ludwig Wittgenstein are incredibly applicable to life itself.

Aside from everything those things that tax my brain in the best possible ways, I love hiking, baseball, basketball, photography, board games, and listening to music of almost every genre.
